# Simple Boats
![Sailboat moored at the shore, sunset background](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/689fb2b908ca868fc88394be1e46f3e0077830b7.jpeg)
Simple Boats adds new powered boats that supplement the vanilla boats for long-range ocean travel. Just get in, unfurl the sail or start the motor, and go. No constant rowing required. Simple as.

Available in all vanilla wood variants!
![All simpleboats variants lined up](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/7d25bff2edfdf077c2a230571f26a2cfe6c98f9d.jpeg)

## Sailboat
![Sailboat sailing by shores](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/095ba2e54fa47fd8a608d27b2ae019c15538f7a4.jpeg)
The humble sailboat brings you at a respectable 10 blocks per second, all while having a large capacity to carry your friends—or some unfortunate villagers for your trading hall.

## Motorboat
![Motorboat sailing by a tavern](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/e134ce5743e346a66e0706121ec7bab6444a24f3.jpeg)
Need to go faster? The motorboat has you covered. Equipped with a powerful motor capable of zipping along at an eye-watering 12 blocks per second, with the same capacity the sailboat offers for a few more resources to craft.
